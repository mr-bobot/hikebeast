Beyond the Border

The spots that aren't in Switzerland. But close enough that leaving them out felt wrong. Chamonix and the Mont Blanc range, plus one view from the French side of Lac Léman. Pack the passport.

14 spots

A RIVER OF ICE ABOVE CHAMONIX

Mer de Glace

The largest glacier in the French Alps. Take the red Montenvers train from Chamonix, then hike up and into the white.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessTrain + 45-60min hike
EffortModerate
Best lightAnytime (sunset / sunrise best)
Open in Maps

THE SAME GLACIER, UNDER SNOW

Mer de Glace. Winter

Same spot, deep winter. Snowshoes on, footprints few, the whole scene turned silent and white.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessTrain + snowshoe hike
EffortModerate. Winter gear
Best lightMorning after fresh snow
Open in Maps

TWIN SPIRES OVER THE GLACIER

Les Drus

Two near-vertical granite spires above the Mer de Glace. From the Montenvers viewpoint, they line up clean against the sky.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessFrom Mer de Glace viewpoint
EffortModerate
Best lightSunset, sunrise, or moody
Open in Maps

THE SPIRES IN WINTER

Les Drus. Winter

Same twin spires, dressed in ice and snow. The contrast against dark granite is what makes the winter shot sing.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessTrain + snowshoe
EffortModerate
Best lightClear winter light

Another frame of Les Drus from the Mer de Glace viewpoint. Same mountain, different compression, different mood.

A POINTED NEEDLE IN THE RANGE

Aiguille du Grépon

A sharp granite needle near the Mer de Glace. A classic alpine silhouette when the light catches the ridge.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessFrom Mer de Glace trail
EffortModerate
Best lightSunset, sunrise, or moody

THE BEST VIEW OF MONT BLANC

Les Chéserys

A cluster of small lakes above La Flégère with the cleanest view of Mont Blanc and the whole surrounding range.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessCable car + hike
EffortModerate
Best lightSunset

HIKE AROUND THE LAKES TO FIND YOUR FRAME

Mighty Range

Same lakes area, different time of day. Daylight walks around Les Chéserys are how you find the compositions for the golden-hour shots.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessSame as Les Chéserys
EffortEasy. Scouting
Best lightSunset or sunrise

BIVY UP HIGH, ABOVE THE CROWDS

Camping in Chéserys

Wild camping at Les Chéserys is allowed. You just need to register online first. Find a quiet spot off the main drag.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessSame as Les Chéserys
EffortModerate
Best lightOvernight (sunset + sunrise)

PROTECTED TERRAIN, WILDLIFE EVERYWHERE

Aiguille Rouge Reserve

The Chéserys area sits inside a protected nature reserve. Ibex, chamois, marmot. Real chances if you stay quiet and patient.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessSame as Les Chéserys
EffortEasy
Best lightEarly morning
Open in Maps

WESTERN EUROPE'S HIGHEST. WITH A TELE

Mont Blanc

From Les Chéserys, Mont Blanc sits enormous across the valley. A telephoto lens turns the view into detail-rich alpine portraiture.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessFrom Les Chéserys
EffortEasy. Standing still
Best lightSunset or blue hour

MY FAVOURITE GLACIER OF THE RANGE

Glacier des Bossons

The Bossons drops farther into the Chamonix valley than any other glacier in the range. Which means you can see it from almost everywhere in town.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessFrom Chamonix valley
EffortEasy
Best lightAny time
