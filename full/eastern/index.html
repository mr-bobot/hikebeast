Eastern Switzerland

Four more from the East

Pinned in my Google Maps but no proper photo for them yet. Worth the detour if you are in the area.

·

MUTTENCHOPF

Mountain peak above the Klausen Pass. Quick scramble for the panorama, drone-friendly.

·

HOHER KASTEN

Appenzell ridge with a cable car to the top. Nice hikes radiate out from the summit station; pick your moment for the light.

·

JULIER PASS

Famous mountain road for photos. Drone work in winter is the move. Snow, silhouettes, big sky.

·

WILDSEE

One of the five lakes on the Pizol trail. Linked to Wildsee by the rest of the loop. Worth a day if you have not been.
