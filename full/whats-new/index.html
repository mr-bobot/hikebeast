What's new

Quick log of what just shipped and what we're working on right now.

Recently shipped

  • Wildcamping section redone May 17

    16 curated spots split by camping style (tent area, tent + bivouac, bivouac only) with a filter chip at the top. Fronalpstock, Moiry Glacier, Gastlosen, and others added.

  • Explore + Home remember where you were May 15

    For You row and the photo wall now stay put when you click into a spot and come back. Reshuffles after an hour, or hit the new Shuffle button on Explore.

  • Dark mode May 13

    Optional dark theme across the whole webapp. Toggle in the rail on desktop, or in the Settings section of the mobile menu.

  • Faster photos, no flicker May 13

    Image grid renders at its final size on first paint instead of reflowing once photos decode. Killed the "image reloads itself" flash on Home and Explore.

  • Submit your own photos May 10

    Any spot page has a button to upload your own shot. We review submissions and add the best ones to the gallery, with photo credit.

Working on now

  • Adding hikes

    Over 100 hikes coming, directly integrated with SwissTopo so you can see the route on the map.

  • Adding public transport

    Public-transport directions for each spot so you know the best train + bus combo to get there.
