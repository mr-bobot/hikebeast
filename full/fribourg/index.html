Fribourg

Cheese country, jagged pre-alps, and a handful of lakes that should be more famous than they are. The canton that looks like the cover of every Swiss guidebook. And still has corners all to yourself.

9 spots

A SEA OF CLOUDS OVER THE PRE-ALPS

Gastlosen

A jagged limestone ridge on the Fribourg-Bern border. Best when an inversion rolls in and the peaks float above the fog.

RegionGruyère, Fribourg
AccessDrive + short hike
EffortEasy
Best lightSunrise with inversion

DINNER WITH A VIEW

Ritzli-Alp

A mountain restaurant on the way back from Gastlosen, with a direct line of sight on the ridge at blue hour.

RegionGruyère, Fribourg
AccessHike or drive
EffortEasy
Best lightSunset into blue hour
Open in Maps

GO WITH A GUIDE THE FIRST TIME

Latine Canyon

A narrow slot canyon near Château-d'Œx. Beautiful water, real risk. This one needs a local before you come back solo.

RegionPays-d'Enhaut
AccessDrive + canyon access
EffortTechnical. Use a guide
Best lightAnytime in shade

A DISTANT PEAK, A LONG LENS

Dent de Broc

Seen from the Moléson road, the Dent lines up just above a meadow. A compressed, minimalist frame if you have the reach.

RegionGruyère, Fribourg
AccessDrive. Roadside stop
EffortNone
Best lightSunrise or sunset
Open in Maps

THE CLASSIC FRIBOURG LAKE

Schwarzsee

A small alpine lake ringed by limestone peaks. Looks like a calendar cover when the sun catches it right.

RegionSense district
AccessDrive to lake
EffortEasy
Best lightSunrise or sunset

ICE HOCKEY, MOUNTAINS, GO

Winter Schwarzsee

Same lake, different season. When the ice is thick enough, you can skate. And play hockey under the peaks.

RegionSense district
AccessDrive to lake
EffortEasy
Best lightDeep winter

A SHARP TRIANGLE ABOVE LES PACCOTS

Dent de Lys

A near-perfect pyramid peak you can hike up from Les Paccots. The ridge is steeper than it looks from the valley.

RegionVeveyse, Fribourg
AccessHike from Les Paccots
EffortModerate. Exposed finish
Best lightSunrise or sunset

HIGH RIDGES NEAR THE CABLE CAR

Moléson

Take the cable car up and you're a few minutes from ridge walks that feel far more remote than they actually are.

RegionGruyère, Fribourg
AccessCable car + short walk
EffortEasy
Best lightSunrise, sunset, or cloudy

Teysachaux

Another Fribourg pyramid. Link it to Moléson along the ridge for a full-day traverse, or park near the base and hike up.

AccessRidge traverse or direct hike
