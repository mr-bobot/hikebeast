Gastlosen

A jagged limestone ridge on the Fribourg-Bern border. Best when an inversion rolls in and the peaks float above the fog.

AccessDrive + short hike
EffortEasy

GO WITH A GUIDE THE FIRST TIME

La Tine Canyon

A narrow slot canyon near Montbovon, marketed out of Château-d'Œx. Beautiful water, real risk. This one needs a local before you come back solo.

RegionPays-d'Enhaut, Fribourg
AccessDrive + canyon access
EffortTechnical. Use a guide
Best lightAnytime in shade

A DISTANT PEAK, A LONG LENS

Dent de Broc

Seen from the Moléson road, the Dent lines up just above a meadow. A compressed, minimalist frame if you have the reach.

RegionGruyère, Fribourg
AccessDrive. Roadside stop
EffortNone
Best lightSunrise or sunset
