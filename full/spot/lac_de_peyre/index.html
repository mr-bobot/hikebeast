A QUIET LAC NEXT TO PIC DE JALLOUVRE

Lac de Peyre

A small alpine lake in the Aravis, same approach as Pic de Jallouvre. Sunset at the Col de Balafrasse is the bonus.

Same approach as Pic de Jallouvre. Start at the Col de la Colombière and take the trail north. The lake sits in a basin below the ridge; bivouac tolerated above the alpages.

For the sunset shot, push a bit further to the Col de Balafrasse. The whole Aravis range lights up while you have the col mostly to yourself.

RegionBornes Massif, Haute-Savoie, France
AccessCol de la Colombière + ridge
EffortModerate
Best lightSunset from Col de Balafrasse
