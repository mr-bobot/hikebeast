13,000 YEARS OF ICE, RETREATING FAST

Saas Fee Glacier

A ski glacier that doubles as a summer photo location. Especially from the rotating restaurant at 3,500m.

Mittelallalin is the reachable high point: cable car from Saas Fee to Felskinn, then the Metro Alpin funicular inside the mountain. The terrace gives you a panorama of the ice fields and the Mischabel peaks. Crowded in ski season, empty in the shoulder months.

If you want to work the glacier edge on foot, the traverse from Spielboden to Längfluh is the best option. A 3-hour route that gets you close to the ice without needing crampons or a guide.
