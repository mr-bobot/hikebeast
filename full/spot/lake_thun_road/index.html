Lake Thun Road

A short stretch of lakeside road where the cliffs come down to the water. Wait for the right car to drive by.

The whole point of this one is composition. Cliff on one side, lake on the other, the road threading the gap. The frame works on its own. It really comes alive when something interesting drives through.

Best on rainy or cloudy days when the asphalt goes wet and reflective. Park safely off the road and shoot from the shoulder. A telephoto compresses everything together.
