AUTUMN LARCHES ON THE SWITCHBACKS

Maloja Pass

The switchback road from Bregaglia up to the Engadin. Autumn larches and long-exposure car headlights make the shot.

Drive up to the viewpoint parking on the Engadin side of the pass. Five minutes from the car to the lip overlooking the switchbacks. No hike.

October is the only month. The larches turn orange-gold and the road snakes through them. Tripod, 8 to 30 second exposure, wait for traffic. Cloudy works; blue hour is best.

How to get there

Drive to viewpoint parking

🚗By car

From St. Moritz follow road 27 southwest along the Engadin lakes (Silvaplana, Sils) to the top of the pass. The viewpoint sits at the start of the hairpins on the Bregaglia side, signed Belvedere.

Parking Maloja Orden parking, free, on the right roughly 100 m after the turn-off. Around 20 spaces, fills fast on autumn weekends.

Walk to spot 5 min

🚆By train + bus

Train to St. Moritz, then PostAuto line 4 (St. Moritz, Maloja, Chiavenna) to the Maloja Poschta stop.

Walk from stop 15 min

⛴By boat

Sils Maria to Maloja passenger boat across Lake Sils. Four runs per day from late June to late September.

🕒When to go

Sunrise for warm light on Piz da la Margna and the Sils basin. Larches turn gold in early October, also the busiest week of the year.

📅Hours / season

Road open year-round, occasionally closed for hours after heavy snow. Boat service late June to late September.
