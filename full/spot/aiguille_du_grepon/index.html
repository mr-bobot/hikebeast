A POINTED NEEDLE IN THE RANGE

Aiguille du Grépon

A sharp granite needle near the Mer de Glace. A classic alpine silhouette when the light catches the ridge.

Visible from the Mer de Glace approach. No separate trek. You'll pass the right angle on the same walk that gets you the glacier shots.

Sunrise and sunset both work. Moody weather with torn cloud gives you the most dramatic rendition. The needle in and out of the mist looks straight out of an old alpinism print.

RegionChamonix, France
AccessFrom Mer de Glace trail
EffortModerate
Best lightSunset, sunrise, or moody
