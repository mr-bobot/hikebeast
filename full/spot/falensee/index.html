LAKES

Fälensee

Saxer Lücke gets the crowds. Drop below the ridge and you find this narrow lake wedged between walls, almost nobody around.

From Saxer Lücke, take the trail south-east and descend toward Bollenwees. Fälensee sits right at the base, a stretched ribbon of water.

The view that sells it is from the ridge above, looking straight down the lake. Telephoto, 85 to 135mm, to compress the walls.

Clouds rolling through the gap at the end of the lake are the bonus. Stick around if you see fog moving in.
