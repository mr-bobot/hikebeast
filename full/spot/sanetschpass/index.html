A PASS WITH A LAKE ON TOP

Sanetschpass

A high mountain pass on the border of Valais and Bern, with a dam, a lake, and a cable car that drops into nothing.

Drive up from Sion through Conthey to the pass at 2,252m. The road is paved but narrow; check it is open (typically late May through October). At the top: the Sanetschsee reservoir, a restaurant, and a very dramatic cable car that descends vertically to Gsteig on the Bern side.

For photos, walk 30 minutes west along the dam toward the open meadows. The ridges catch morning light cleanly. A drone works well up here. No crowds, no airspace restrictions to worry about.
