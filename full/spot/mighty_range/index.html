HIKE AROUND THE LAKES TO FIND YOUR FRAME

Mighty Range

Same lakes area, different time of day. Daylight walks around Les Chéserys are how you find the compositions for the golden-hour shots.

Walk around the lake system during the day to scout compositions. Find the rocks, the reflections, the foreground frames that will work when the light turns. Then come back at sunset or sunrise fully prepared.

Both sunset and sunrise work here. Sunrise puts first light on the range; sunset is warmer but with haze risk from a full day's heating. Morning is usually the cleaner shot.
