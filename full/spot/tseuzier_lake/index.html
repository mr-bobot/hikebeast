A LAKE THAT REFLECTS LIKE GLASS

Tseuzier Lake

A reservoir behind Crans-Montana. When the wind drops, the water goes still enough to mirror the peaks on the far shore.

Drive up the Rawyl road from Ayent. The lake sits at the top. Park at the dam, then walk the path along the north shore. The best reflections are at sunrise in late October when the larches on the surrounding slopes turn gold.

Wide lens for the whole vista, or a tele to isolate the reflected ridge. Mornings are almost always calmer than afternoons here; if the wind is up, walk to the far end where the water is more sheltered.
