Oeschinensee

The famous turquoise lake above Kandersteg. Not a hidden gem anymore, but if you walk past the platform it gets quiet fast.

Almost every reel I ever recorded here went viral. Postcard Switzerland. Creators like myself made it crowded. Not a hidden gem, still worth a visit if you do it right.

Plan a whole day. Walk up the hill on the left side of the lake, past where most people stop. Bring cash because card payments do not work at the restaurants near the gondola.

You need decent weather for the lake to be that blue. Cloudy days kill the colour.

RegionKandersteg, Central Switzerland
AccessGondola + 20 min walk
EffortEasy
