THE CLASSIC FRIBOURG LAKE

Schwarzsee

A small alpine lake ringed by limestone peaks. Looks like a calendar cover when the sun catches it right.

Arrive at sunrise, before the road fills up. The lake is quiet for maybe 45 minutes after first light. After that, the day trippers start showing up.

Don't shoot when the sun is high. You'll lose all contrast. Keep the sun low in your frame, or wait for cloud cover to soften the scene.
