MY FAVOURITE GLACIER OF THE RANGE

Glacier des Bossons

The Bossons drops farther into the Chamonix valley than any other glacier in the range. Which means you can see it from almost everywhere in town.

Visible from the Chamonix valley floor. Many angles work; pick a foreground. A chalet, a treeline, a bend of the river. And anchor the glacier in it.

Anytime light is fine. Late afternoon warms the ice and catches the highlights. A good viewpoint is from above, on the Aiguille du Midi cable car route. Or with a drone if you're somewhere you can fly legally.
