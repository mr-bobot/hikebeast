RIDGES

Hardergrat Trail

The big one. Twelve hours along a knife-edge ridge from Brienzer Rothorn to Harder Kulm, no water the whole way.

Take the cable car up to the Brienzer Rothorn (either from Brienz or from Sörenberg) and start the hike there. It ends at Harder Kulm above Interlaken.

The whole hike takes about twelve hours and has no water sources. Carry everything you need. Not an easy hike. Take it seriously.
