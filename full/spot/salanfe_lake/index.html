A HIDDEN LAKE IN A HANGING VALLEY

Salanfe Lake

A reservoir that sits in a bowl between the Dents du Midi. Harder to reach than it looks on the map. That is the point.

Accessible via Van d'en Haut above Salvan. Park where the road ends and hike about 2 hours up. Alternative: approach from Mex in the Rhône valley, longer but quieter. Either way, the climb opens out suddenly to a huge still lake ringed by walls of rock.

Stay overnight at the Cabane de Salanfe if you can book it. Dawn on the lake is among the best in the canton. A drone works well up here but the winds can pick up fast, so keep your eye on the treeline and do not fly far.
