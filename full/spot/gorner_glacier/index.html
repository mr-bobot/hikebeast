GLACIERS

Gorner Glacier

Scale is the whole story here. You want the biggest frame you can get your hands on.

Best from above. Fly a drone where the rules let you, or book a scenic flight out of Raron if you want the real panoramic. From the ground, the Gornergrat platform is the easy option.

From Gornergrat, a telephoto compresses the ice in a way a wide angle just cannot match. 100 to 400mm isolates the texture of the crevasses.

Go on a clear day. Fresh snow hides everything; clear light lets the blue in the ice read.
