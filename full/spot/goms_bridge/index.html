BRIDGES

Goms Bridge

A smaller hanging bridge in the upper Goms valley - less famous than Randa.

A quick half-hour detour if you are driving through the Goms on your way to or from Grimsel. Easy walk, solid views, and fewer crowds than the famous Randa bridge.

Good for a quick adrenaline hit without a full day commitment.
