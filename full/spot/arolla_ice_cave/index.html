Arolla Ice Cave

A hike to a cave carved into the ice - remarkable in person.

Park at coordinates 46.020048, 7.481951 and hike to the cave at 45.992908, 7.494244. The cave collapsed in September 2022 after a small Val d'Hérens earthquake.

The bowl and the moraine still hold up as a hike. Bring warm layers either way.
