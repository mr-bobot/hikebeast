ICE HOCKEY, MOUNTAINS, GO

Winter Schwarzsee

Same lake, different season. When the ice is thick enough, you can skate. And play hockey under the peaks.

Call the tourism office before you drive up. The ice needs to be checked every year; they'll tell you if it's safe. Some winters it's perfect, some it never fully freezes.

Great with friends. Bring skates, a couple of sticks, a puck. There's no more Swiss scene than hockey on a natural rink with big walls of limestone around you.
