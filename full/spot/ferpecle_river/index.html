Ferpècle River

The meltwater braid downstream of the glacier. From a drone, it looks painted.

Same approach as the glacier. On the way in or out, get airborne above the flats where the river fans across the gravel bed. Grey silt, pale blue channels, and sharp dark lines where the water bites into the sediment.

Shoot top-down between 10am and 2pm for the cleanest separation between water and stone. The pattern changes through the day as the glacier melts faster in the afternoon, so you can revisit the same spot and get a different frame.
