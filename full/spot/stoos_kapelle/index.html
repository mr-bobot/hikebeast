CHAPELS

Stoos Kapelle

A small wooden chapel on a green knoll above the car-free Stoos village, with the Mythen rising behind.

Stoos is the car-free village reached by the Stoosbahn funicular from Schwyz-Schlattli. Walk a few minutes from the village to the Stooskapelle Maria Hilf.

Time it for afternoon when the clouds pile up behind the peaks. The chapel in the bottom third, a big sky on top.

The detail that makes it work is the dark wood against the grey limestone of the Mythen. Don't oversaturate it. Let the contrast do the talking.

RegionStoos, Schwyz
AccessFunicular to Stoos + 5 min walk
