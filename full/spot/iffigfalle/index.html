WATERFALLS

Iffigfalle

Above Lenk, best in May and June when snowmelt pushes the falls into their fullest.

The waterfall is close to the road but the best angle requires a quick hike above the parking area. May and June is peak water time - if you come later, plan for smaller volume.

Overcast light works well here. The falls freeze in winter but are less dramatic without the snowmelt pressure.
