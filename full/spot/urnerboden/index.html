LIMESTONE THAT LOOKS ITALIAN

Urnerboden

A stretch of peaks above Urnerboden on the Klausenpass road. Jagged, bone-grey limestone that honestly reminds me of Italy.

Park along the pass road and walk up a short ridge. The peaks above the Urnerboden valley have the kind of jagged limestone that looks lifted from the Dolomites, with zero crowd.

Sunrise or moody overcast both deliver. Blue-sky blue-hour days are actually the weakest here. The rock lacks contrast against a clean sky.

RegionUrnerboden, Uri
AccessPass road + short hike
EffortEasy
