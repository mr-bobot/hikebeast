SOFT LIGHT, LONG EXPOSURES

Berschnerfall

A tall waterfall near Walenstadt. Cloudy days flatter it; bring a tripod and a blurred foreground.

Compose with something in the foreground. Moss, ferns, a wet branch. For depth. The classic mistake is shooting the falls head-on with no lead-in element.

Watch your highlights. The white of the water clips fast even on overcast days. Underexpose slightly, recover in post.
