OLD WOOD, SURREAL LIGHT

Zermatt huts

A little hamlet of old wooden houses above Zermatt. A ghost town only in winter.

From Zermatt, hike to the end of the village and then up toward the cable car station at Furi. You'll find this little hamlet of old wooden houses, only a ghost town in winter when the restaurant is closed.

Go at late afternoon or on an overcast day. Hard sun blows out the wood texture; soft light makes the timber glow. The Matterhorn pokes up behind if the sky cooperates.

RegionZermatt, Valais
AccessHike up from Zermatt
EffortEasy
