Le Doubs

A cliff-top view of the Doubs river canyon, above the village of Les Brenets.

Park at the Restaurant Les Roches de Moron and walk to the cliffs above the river. The view drops onto the canyon and the river bend below.

This is a Jura spot, windier and moodier than alpine areas. Overcast light really suits it, or catch the sunset when the canyon walls glow.

RegionLes Brenets, Western Switzerland
AccessDrive + short walk
EffortEasy
Best lightOvercast or sunset
