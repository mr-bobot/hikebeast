IF YOU HAVE THE LEVEL. OR A FRIEND WHO DOES

Valle Verzasca Kayak

The Verzasca is famous for whitewater kayaking. Not something you just try. But if you have the skill, or a friend who does, the shots are unreal.

The valley holds serious whitewater. Only experienced kayakers should be on the water here; the rocks and currents don't forgive mistakes. If you're shooting and not paddling, team up with a kayaker who knows the lines.

A drone captures the scale. Turquoise water, bleached boulders, a tiny kayak tracking through the curves. Mid to late summer is when flow eases to navigable levels; spring runoff is expert-only.

RegionValle Verzasca, Ticino
AccessAlong the valley road
