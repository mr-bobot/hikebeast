GLACIER LAKES

Triftsee

The turquoise lake behind the bridge. Same hike, different light, the place where I would actually spend the night.

I recommend camping up there if you want to catch both sunset and sunrise. The cable car does not run early enough or late enough, so camping is the only way to get those shots.

Set up above the treeline, stay discreet, leave no trace. The lake goes from glass-still in the morning to glacier-fed current by afternoon.

RegionGadmen, Central Switzerland
AccessCable car + 2 hour hike
EffortModerate
Best lightSunset or sunrise
