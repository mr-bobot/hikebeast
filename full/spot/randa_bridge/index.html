BRIDGES

Randa Bridge

Switzerland longest hanging bridge - a wobbling half-hour crossing.

The bridge sits between Visp and Zermatt in Randa. It swings more than you might expect, but the crossing is not technical - just commitment.

Short drive detour, big photo moment. Visit on a clear day so you can actually see where the valley opens up below.
