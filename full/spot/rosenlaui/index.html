Rosenlaui

Tucked into the Reichenbach valley, where the Wetterhorn feels unreal in the late afternoon light.

No need to go all the way up to Rosenlaui village. The plateau five minutes before makes the best image of the mountain in the back. The Wetterhorn. Framed by pines and meadow.
