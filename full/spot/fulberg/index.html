HIDDEN GEMS

Fulberg

A ridge hike above Lake Uri that almost nobody does. Wide open, quiet, and the photo pays for the climb.

Take the Niederbauen-Klewenalp cable car up from Emmetten, then follow the ridge trail. It is a steady climb with no technical sections, but the ridge exposes you to weather fast, so pick a clear window.

Plan to stay late or sleep up there. The light two hours before sunset is when the ridge reads best. Bring a shell, water, and layers even in summer.

If you want the frame you see here, walk past the first viewpoint and keep going. The first bench is the crowded one. The second is empty.
