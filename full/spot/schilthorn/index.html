SUMMITS

Schilthorn

Piz Gloria. Cable car straight to a 360 degree view of the Bernese Alps. The Eiger, Mönch and Jungfrau line up across the valley.

Take the cable car all the way up to Piz Gloria. The view from the terrace is hard to beat, mountains in every direction, with the Eiger, Mönch and Jungfrau standing in a row across the valley.

Drone shots work especially well from the saddle just below the summit station. Sunset is the one to chase. The light catches the snowfields and the whole face turns gold.

RegionMürren, Central Switzerland
AccessCable car from Stechelberg via Mürren
EffortEasy
Best lightSunset
