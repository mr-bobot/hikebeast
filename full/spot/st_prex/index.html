CLIFF JUMPING INTO LAKE GENEVA

St-Prex

A pebble shoreline on the north side of Léman with a wooden jumping platform. A short train from Lausanne.

GPS: 46.478432, 6.456433. It's a summer spot. Warm water, deep, with a great backdrop of the French Alps across the lake. Check the depth yourself before any big jump.

Afternoon is when the sun hits the water right. Bring a waterproof camera or GoPro. The platform is the main draw but there are lower ledges for people easing in.

RegionLa Côte, Vaud
