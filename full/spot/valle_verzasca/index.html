THE WHOLE VALLEY IS THE SPOT

Valle Verzasca

Turquoise river, bleached-white boulders, all the way up the valley. Pick any pull-off and you'll find something.

The whole Valle Verzasca is worth driving end to end. Rock formations along the river create natural pools, small jumps, swimming holes. Keep your eye out as you drive.

CURRENT IS SERIOUS. People drown in this river every summer because they underestimate the flow after rain. Check conditions, don't jump into water you haven't scouted.

The river is also famous for whitewater kayaking, expert-only. From above, a drone shot of a kayak tracking through the curves is worth the wait.
