Fronalpstock

Lake Lucerne laid out below, sunset turning the whole basin pink. Cable car up from Morschach, a short walk to the bench.

Take the cable car from Morschach Talstation up to Fronalpstock. Walk ten minutes along the ridge to the main viewpoint. Stone bench, zero work.

Aim for sunset. The sun drops right behind Mount Pilatus and the light catches the full length of the lake. In winter the snow cover pushes the contrast even further.

Wide first, then swap to a longer lens for the compressed version of Pilatus and the Vierwaldstättersee fingers.
