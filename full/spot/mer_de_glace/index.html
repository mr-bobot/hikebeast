A RIVER OF ICE ABOVE CHAMONIX

Mer de Glace

The largest glacier in the French Alps. Take the red Montenvers train from Chamonix, then hike up and into the white.

From Chamonix, catch the Montenvers train to its upper station. The view takes in the whole glacier, with markers along the descent showing where the ice was in 1985, 2000, and 2010. A new telecabine (Feb 2024) drops to ice level; the moraine trail (45 to 60 min) holds the better vantages.

Anytime light works. Sunset, sunrise, moody overcast, or a clear night for stars. Bring warm layers; the wind coming off the ice strips the heat fast even in summer.

In winter the trail goes silent and white. Snowshoes are mandatory and the Montenvers schedule is reduced, last train down around 14:30, line closed for engineering in November and May.
