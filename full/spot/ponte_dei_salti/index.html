THE ARCH, THE JUMP, THE ICON

Ponte dei Salti

The double-arched stone bridge in Lavertezzo. Around fifteen metres up, cold river underneath. Send it if you're up for it.

Park in Lavertezzo and walk to the bridge. The jump is about 15 meters and the water is freezing even in August. Check the river level and for any rocks before you commit.

Anytime light is fine. The canyon shade keeps contrast manageable most of the day. Early morning is quietest. Late afternoon gets the most swimmers and best atmosphere.
