Augstmatthorn

Ridge view over Brienzersee. Same trail as the Hardergrat, but you can sneak in from the back.

On the Hardergrat ridge but accessible from the other side too. Park at Lombachalp in Habkern and do the two hour hike up. Lighter than the full traverse, same view.

Great panorama over the Brienzersee. For the lake to pop you want clean weather, but cloudy days still work if you frame the ridge against the moving sky.

RegionHabkern, Central Switzerland
Access2h hike from Lombachalp
EffortModerate
