THE SKI STATION IN SUMMER

Crans-Montana

Off-season, the empty slopes look like someone drained a golf course and left it. In the best possible way.

From Crans-Montana village, take any of the lifts up (most run summer service). The interesting photos are on the upper slopes above 2,000m. Wide-open meadows, scattered chalets, and long sightlines toward the Valais Alps across the valley.

I come here for moody morning fog that rolls up the slope and disappears by 10am. The Plaine Morte glacier is just above. Worth a full-day visit if the weather holds. Avoid weekends in mid-summer; it is crowded with Swiss families.
