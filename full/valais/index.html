Six more from the Valais

Pinned in my Google Maps but no proper photos for them yet. Worth the detour if you are in the area.

·

LAC BLEU

Tiny turquoise pool near Arolla. Short hike, big colour. The lake is small so the frame works close.

·

HÄNGEBRÜCKE FÜRGANGEN - MÜHLEBACH

Hanging bridge in the upper Goms valley. Quick to visit, and a good frame on a moody day.

·

VAN D'EN BAS

Hamlet above Salvan with a clean line of sight on the Mont Blanc range. Drive up, walk a few minutes, frame the peaks.

·

SANETSCH PASS

Mountain pass accessible by car. Beginning of winter is the window — snowy arctic vibes before the road closes for the season.

·

LAC DE DERBORENCE

Quiet alpine lake formed by an old rockslide. Late afternoon light catches the cliff face on the far shore.

·

ZINAL GLACIER

End of the Val d'Anniviers. Long approach from Zinal village or via the Cabane des Bossons. Glacier reads bigger from the moraine ridge.
