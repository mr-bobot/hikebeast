Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 May 2026

This page explains what data we collect when you visit Hikebeast and how we use it. We keep it short on purpose. If you have questions, email leon@hikebeast.ch.

Who we are

Saftladen GmbH, Birkenstrasse 47, 6343 Rotkreuz, Switzerland. Contact: leon@hikebeast.ch. See our imprint for full company details.

Email sign-ups

If you request the free sample of the Swiss Gems of Switzerland guide, we store your email address so we can send you the guide and occasional follow-up messages about new editions or related products. You can unsubscribe from any of those emails at any time, which removes your address from the list.

Services we use

The site uses the following third-party services. Some set cookies in your browser, others only process data when you take a specific action like requesting the sample or buying the guide. Each provider has its own privacy policy linked below.

ManyChat pixel

We use the ManyChat pixel to connect this website with our Instagram DM funnel. When you arrive from one of our DMs, ManyChat can recognise you and attribute which conversation brought you here. The pixel sets cookies from the manychat.com and mccdn.me domains.

Privacy details: manychat.com/privacy.html

Facebook / Meta pixel

We use the Meta Pixel (operated by Meta Platforms Ireland Ltd.) to measure how visitors interact with the site, retarget people who have shown interest, and improve ads on Facebook and Instagram. The pixel sets cookies from facebook.com.

Privacy details: facebook.com/privacy/policy

Meta Conversions API (server-side)

In addition to the browser Pixel, we send purchase events server-side to Meta via their Conversions API. This includes hashed versions of your email and the order amount, sent without cookies, so Meta can attribute the purchase to ad campaigns even if your browser blocks the Pixel. Data is hashed (SHA-256) before transmission.

Details: facebook.com/business/help/conversions-api

Stripe (payment processing)

When you buy the Swiss Gems of Switzerland guide, payment runs through Stripe (operated by Stripe Payments Europe Ltd. for European customers). Stripe receives your card or wallet details directly through their embedded checkout, and we never see or store full card numbers. Stripe also receives your email and billing country to issue the receipt and prevent fraud. They store this data on their own servers, governed by their own privacy policy.

Privacy details: stripe.com/privacy

Resend (email delivery)

We use Resend to send transactional emails: the purchase confirmation with your download link, and the free-sample email if you signed up for one. Resend processes the recipient address and message content, and keeps delivery logs for a limited time so we can investigate if an email bounces or doesn't arrive.

Privacy details: resend.com/legal/privacy-policy

Vercel (hosting)

This site is hosted on Vercel. Their infrastructure logs basic request data (IP address, user agent, page URL, response code) for short-term operational monitoring. We also run Vercel Analytics, which counts page views without using cookies.

Privacy details: vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

Order log (Google Sheets via Apps Script)

After a successful purchase, we log the order metadata to a private Google Sheet via Google Apps Script: order id, your email, currency, amount, and the country code Stripe inferred from your IP. We use it for our own bookkeeping. The personal data overlaps with what Stripe already holds on your behalf.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold about you. Send an email to leon@hikebeast.ch and we'll handle it within a reasonable time.

Cookies

You can block cookies in your browser settings. Blocking the cookies above may prevent some functionality (for example, email delivery of the free sample) but will not affect the rest of the site.
