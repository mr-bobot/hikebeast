Last updated: 22 April 2026
Source page: https://hikebeast.ch/privacy.html

This page explains what data we collect when you visit Hikebeast and how we use it. We keep it short on purpose. Questions: office@gorped.com.

## Who we are

## Email sign-ups

If you request the free sample of the Swiss Hidden Gems guide, we store your email address so we can send you the guide and occasional follow-up messages about new editions or related products. You can unsubscribe from any of those emails at any time, which removes your address from the list.

## Third-party tools

The site loads tracking and chat tools from the following providers. Each sets cookies or similar identifiers when you interact with the page.

### ManyChat pixel

We use the ManyChat pixel to connect this website with our Instagram DM funnel. When you arrive from one of our DMs, ManyChat can recognise you and attribute which conversation brought you here. The pixel sets cookies from the manychat.com and mccdn.me domains. Privacy details: https://manychat.com/privacy.html.

### Facebook / Meta pixel

We use the Meta Pixel (operated by Meta Platforms Ireland Ltd.) to measure how visitors interact with the site, retarget people who have shown interest, and improve ads on Facebook and Instagram. The pixel sets cookies from facebook.com. Privacy details: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/.

## Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold about you. Send an email to office@gorped.com and we will handle it within a reasonable time.

## Cookies

You can block cookies in your browser settings. Blocking the cookies above may prevent some functionality (for example, email delivery of the free sample) but will not affect the rest of the site.
